About Me
Amelie “Lia” Mintz is a Brazilian graphic designer and artist who expresses a profound dedication to her craft. With affinity for the Y2K aesthetic, she infuses her creations with vibrant nostalgia and innovative designs. However, Mintz's artistic pursuits transcend the trends; they reflect a quest for depth and meaning. Beyond the superficial allure of aesthetics, she seeks to evoke genuine emotion with her designs.
